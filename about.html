Who Are We?

Minako is the newest leading online shopping platform in Oman. Developed in 2023, its vast product collection includes exclusive collections, formal, casual, and sports couture, and globally recognized brands. We pride ourselves on the range and price of our products, which are 100% genuine products, and we comply with the National Consumer Protection Act.

Minako has grown into a brand that champions digital innovation, has a fiercely independent spirit and inspires its fashion loving customer to experiment with their style.
